# The Mist

## One-line function
The pressure from outside: a supernatural medium that carries demons and erodes memory.

## Ratified facts
- The Mist is not weather; it has intent or carries intent.
- Prolonged exposure distorts memory and, in some, lends a borrowed voice.
- Incursions trigger civic curfew and gate-sealing protocols.

## Open questions
- What does the Mist want, if anything?
- Why do children seem more susceptible?

## Story utility
- Source of dread and the lie the survival apparatus tells about it.
