# The Volcanic City-State

## One-line function
The setting's body: a working city built into and against a living volcano.

## Ratified facts
- Built into the inner and outer rim of an active caldera.
- Survival depends on heat infrastructure and a civic defense apparatus.
- Class maps loosely to altitude: rim is power, lower terraces are labor.

## Open questions
- Is the volcano dormant, semi-active, or ritually controlled?
- Who is legally admitted to the upper rim?

## Story utility
- Power center and ritual spectacle; where civic survival and elite hypocrisy collide.
