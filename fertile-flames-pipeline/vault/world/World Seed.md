# Volcanic City-State Battling Demons and Mist

## Core premise
A fortified volcanic city-state survives between infernal pressure from below and a supernatural mist beyond its walls. Its survival apparatus is competent, brutal, and lying about something.

## Primary tensions
- Heat, ash, industry, ritual, and civic order inside the city.
- Mist, demons, contagion, and memory-distortion outside it.
- Factions disagree on whether survival demands faith, engineering, sacrifice, or appeasement.

## Tone
Grounded, administratively real, slightly mythic. Civic horror over action spectacle.

## Hard constraints
- The city-state must feel administratively real.
- Demons pressure society, economy, theology, and law — not just bodies.
- The Mist is more than weather.
- Volcanic geography shapes daily life, defense, industry, and class.
