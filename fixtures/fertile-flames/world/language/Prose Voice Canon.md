# Prose Voice Canon

## Preferred
- Concrete sensory detail tied to material culture.
- Dry, implied humor; administrative horror.
- Mythic scale grounded in practical systems.

## Avoid
- Generic fantasy archaism.
- Overexplaining the magic.
- Banter in a modern register.
- "A chill ran down her spine."
- Characters narrating worldbuilding at each other.

## Exemplars
> The ration clerk stamped her docket twice, which meant the charcoal was real and the weight was not.
