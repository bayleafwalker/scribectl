# Episode 1.1 — The Ditch Song

## Brief
A spoken-fic episode for the ear: the tutorial premise as story. Ilmi digs
to the work song, notices the strokes land truer on the beat, and — once,
briefly, by accident — the meadow answers back.

## Required beats
- The work song exists to make digging bearable; nobody calls it magic.
- Her stroke lands on the beat and the ditch takes it differently.
- She loses the verse, rests the empty beats, and re-enters on the phrase.
- Something in the meadow answers one line — and she tells no one.

## Exit state
Ilmi knows the beat matters and has told no one; the charred tulen synty
page is still just a keepsake to her.

## Tone target
Work-worn wonder; the fifth beat felt before it is understood.

## Do not
- Explain the väki or name them aloud.
- Let anyone cast a deliberate spell.
- Mention the game, lanes, or timing windows in any form.
