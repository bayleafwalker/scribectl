# Prose Voice Canon

## Preferred
- Concrete work first, wonder second: hands on the hoe before the väki stir.
- spoken_fic: short clauses that breathe on the fifth beat; read-aloud test
  every paragraph.
- blog_post: first person, craft-forward, cite the tradition by name.

## Avoid
- Mechanics leaking as mechanics — never "judgment window", always what the
  singer hears and feels.
- Mysticism fog: the väki are literal-minded, not ineffable.

## Exemplars
The ditch took her stroke on the beat and gave nothing back on the off. Ilmi
counted five without meaning to; the meadow counted with her.

She stopped singing at the burned place and let the beats pass empty, one,
two, and came back in on the phrase like stepping onto a floating log —
weight only where the song could hold it.
