# Ilmi

## One-line function
The apprentice: a ditch-digger who learned that strokes land truer on the
beat long before anyone told her that was magic.

## Ratified facts
- Ilmi learned the work songs first; kuokkiminen to a beat taught her the
  pulse before any loitsu did.
- She carries a charred page of tulen synty with its third virke burned away.
- She has never yet sung to the väki of anything larger than a cooking fire.

## Open questions
- Who burned the page, and did they burn the verse on purpose?

The tutorial premise made a person — every early tie-in can sit inside her
learning curve.
