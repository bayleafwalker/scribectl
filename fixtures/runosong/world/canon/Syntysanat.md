# Syntysanat

## One-line function
Origin-words: power over a thing comes from knowing and reciting its birth —
the load-bearing rule that collapses the magic system and the knowledge
system into one thing.

## Ratified facts
- Command over a thing requires singing its origin: raudan synty for iron,
  tulen synty for fire.
- There is no spell list; a fragment about the birth of iron is a weapon
  against anything made of iron.
- An incomplete origin leaves a hole in the song: the singer must stay
  silent on the missing beats — rests as difficulty.
- Tietäjä power is knowing more words: additional virket lengthen the loop
  before it resolves, trading cast speed for effect.

## Open questions
- Can a partial synty be completed by hearing an enemy sing the rest?

## Story utility
Fragments are found lore (Spiderweb-style); a charred page with a verse
missing is both plot object and difficulty modifier.
